## THE 7 INCOME STREAMS - COMPLETE BREAKDOWN

### 1. ISP (Individual Sales Profit) - Monthly
- **Bronze Sponsors:** 25%
- **Copper Sponsors:** 30%
- **Silver Sponsors:** 35%
- **Gold Sponsors:** 40%
- **Platinum Sponsors:** 45%
- **Trigger:** Immediate on every sale
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Risk Level:** HIGH (guaranteed on every sale)

### 2. QPB (Quick Pathfinder Bonus) - Monthly (First 90 Days Only)
- **First 3 invited builders:** 7.5%
- **Subsequent sets of 3:** 10%
- **Trigger:** Every builder invited in first 90 days
- **Frequency:** Monthly (temporary)
- **Risk Level:** MEDIUM (time-limited, creates urgency)

### 3. TSC (Team Sales Commission) - Monthly
- **G2:** 10%
- **G3:** 5%
- **G4:** 3%
- **G5:** 2%
- **G6-G10:** 1% each (5% total)
- **TOTAL:** 25% (if all 10 generations qualified)
- **Trigger:** Every sale in downline up to 10 generations
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Risk Level:** VERY HIGH (cumulative, cascading payouts)

### 4. TLI (Team Leadership Incentive) - Quarterly
- **Pool Size:** 10% of Total Member TeamPV
- **Distribution:** Hybrid (minimum guarantee + pool bonus)
- **Trigger:** Quarterly based on qualifications
- **Frequency:** Quarterly (3.33% monthly equivalent)
- **Risk Level:** MEDIUM (pool-based, controlled)

### 5. CEA (CEO Awards) - Quarterly
- **Type:** Luxury trips, cash bonuses, recognition
- **Budget:** Variable (CEO approved)
- **Trigger:** Performance-based
- **Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Risk Level:** LOW (discretionary, controlled)

### 6. CEC (CEO Competitions) - Quarterly
- **Type:** Performance competitions, prizes
- **Budget:** Variable (pre-set competition budgets)
- **Trigger:** Competition winners only
- **Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Risk Level:** LOW (fixed budgets, few winners)

### 7. MKT (Marketplace Sales) - Monthly
- **Platform Fee:** 5%
- **Seller Keeps:** 95%
- **Trigger:** Marketplace product sales only
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Risk Level:** NEGLIGIBLE (separate product sales, profit center)
