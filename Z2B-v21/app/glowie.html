Pro Tips: How to Prompt GLOWIE Like a Boss

DO This:

  • Be specific: "Build a fitness tracker with calorie counter, workout timer, and progress charts"
  • Mention colors: "Use blue and white theme with modern gradients"
  • Define features: "Include dark mode toggle, save data locally, mobile responsive"
  • State the purpose: "For busy professionals who need quick meal planning"

DON'T Do This:

  • "Make me an app" - Too vague!
  • "Something for fitness" - Not specific enough
  • "Build it like that other app" - GLOWIE doesn't know which one
  • "Just do whatever" - Be the director of your vision!
