Sales Targets are executive-driven goals designed to boost revenue and drive specific business outcomes. Unlike Competitions (which offer prizes), Targets focus on achieving sales milestones with special awards for qualifiers.

  • Target Specific Tiers: Focus on specific membership levels or all members

  • Special Awards: Recognize and reward top performers
